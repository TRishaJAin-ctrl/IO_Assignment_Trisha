APPLICATIONS OF STACKS:-

1. Stacks could be used for reversing a string.

2. They could also be used for backtracking.

3. Stacks are also used for natural language processing.

4. Undo mechanisms in text editors use stacks.

5. Stacks are used in depth first search.

APPLICATIONS OF QUEUES:-

1. Queues can be used in breadth first search.

2. They are useful in CPU scheduling and Disk Scheduling. 

3. They are also used when data is transferred asynchronously between two processes.

4. Handling of interrupts in real-time systems. 
   The interrupts are handled in the same order as they arrive, First come first served.
   
5. Queues are also used in recognising palindrome.
